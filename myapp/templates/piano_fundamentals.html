Piano Fundamentals

Understand the layout of the piano keyboard and how notes are arranged. Learn the names of white and black keys with easy identification methods.
Develop proper hand positioning for comfortable and accurate playing.

1. Understanding the Piano Layout

  • Black keys come in groups of 2 and 3
  • White key left of 2 black keys = C
  • Sequence: C D E F G A B

2. Proper Hand Position

  • Keep fingers curved
  • Keep wrist straight
  • Use finger numbers

3. C Major Scale

Finger pattern:

1 → 2 → 3 → 1 → 2 → 3 → 4 → 5
