Chords & Accompaniment

Understand major and minor chords and basic rhythm patterns.
Learn how to build chords and play simple progressions.

1. Major Chords

  • Major chords sound happy and bright
  • Formula: Root + 3rd + 5th
  • Example: C - E - G

2. Minor Chords

  • Minor chords sound sad or emotional
  • Formula: Root + flat 3rd + 5th
  • Example: A - C - E

3. Chord Progression

  • Practice simple progressions like C - G - Am - F
  • Keep rhythm steady
  • Use left hand for chords
