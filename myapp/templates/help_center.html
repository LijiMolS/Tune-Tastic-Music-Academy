Help Center

Find answers to questions about Tune Tastic Music Academy, your courses, subscriptions, and account.

1. Account & Profile

How do I create an account?

Click on the "Register" button on the homepage, fill in your details (username, email, password), and submit. You will then be able to access free courses immediately.

How do I edit my profile?

Go to your Profile page and click "Edit Profile". You can update your name, email, profile picture, and other personal details.

What if I forget my password?

On the Login page, click "Forgot Password". Enter your registered email and follow the instructions sent to reset your password.

2. Courses & Subscriptions

How do I enroll in a course?

Select a course (Free, Standard, or Premium), click "Subscribe", complete the payment (if applicable), and your course will appear in your profile.

How do I access my courses?

All your enrolled courses are listed under your Profile → My Courses. Click on any course to start learning immediately.

Can I upgrade or cancel my subscription?

Go to Profile → Subscription. You can upgrade to a higher plan or cancel your subscription. Upgrades take effect immediately; cancellations remain active until the end of the billing cycle.

3. Lessons & Materials

How can I access lesson materials?

Click on your course → Module → Lesson. Each lesson includes videos, PDFs, and practice exercises. Progress is saved automatically.

Can I download resources?

Some course materials are downloadable. Check the lesson page for a download button beside PDFs and sheet music.

4. Support & Troubleshooting

How do I contact Tune Tastic support?

Use the "Contact Us" form on the website. Provide your name, email, and message. Our team usually responds within 24–48 hours.

What if a video or lesson isn’t loading?

Check your internet connection. If the problem persists, clear your browser cache or try another browser. Contact support if the issue continues.

How do I track my progress?

Your progress is tracked automatically in each course. Completed lessons will show a checkmark, and you can view your overall course completion in your Profile.

How do I get a certificate?

After completing a course, a "Download Certificate" button will appear on the course page. You can download and print your certificate anytime.
