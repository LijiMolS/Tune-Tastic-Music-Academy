Songs & Performance

Improve rhythm, timing and expression while performing complete songs.
Build confidence and musical expression.

1. Play a Song with Both Hands

  • Combine melody and chords
  • Keep steady rhythm
  • Start slow, then increase speed

2. Dynamics Practice (Soft / Loud)

  • Play soft (piano) and loud (forte)
  • Control finger pressure
  • Add emotion to your playing

3. Metronome Timing Practice

  • Practice with steady tempo
  • Improve timing accuracy
  • Stay consistent throughout
