Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how Tune Tastic Music Academy collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

We collect information you provide when creating an account, enrolling in courses, submitting contact forms, or using other platform features. This may include your name, email, payment details, and course progress.

2. How We Use Information

Your information is used to provide and improve our services, process payments, communicate important updates, and personalize your learning experience.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with trusted service providers who help us operate the platform, and when required by law.

4. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance user experience, track usage patterns, and provide relevant course recommendations.

5. Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security practices to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or modification.

6. Data Retention

Your personal information is retained only as long as necessary to provide services, comply with legal obligations, or resolve disputes.

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. Contact our support team for any requests regarding your data.

Our platform may contain links to external sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party websites.

9. Children’s Privacy

Our platform is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under this age.

10. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page, and continued use of our platform constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
