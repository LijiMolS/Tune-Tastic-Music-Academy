Chords & Harmony

Understand chord structures and harmony to accompany melodies effectively.
Learn how chords work together to create rich musical sound.

1. Major & Minor Chords

  • Major chords sound happy
  • Minor chords sound sad
  • Example: C Major = C E G

2. Chord Transitions

  • Practice smooth movement between chords
  • Common progression: C → G → Am → F
  • Keep fingers close to keys

3. Harmonizing Melodies

  • Add chords to simple melodies
  • Use left hand for chords
  • Right hand plays melody
