Keyboard Fundamentals

Learn the keyboard layout, note names, and proper finger positioning. Easily identify white and black
keys and start playing the C major scale.

1. Identify White & Black Keys

  • Black keys are grouped in 2s and 3s
  • White key before 2 black keys = C
  • Pattern repeats across the keyboard

2. Finger Placement

  • Thumb = 1, Index = 2, Middle = 3
  • Keep fingers curved
  • Relax your wrist

3. C Major Scale

Right Hand Finger Pattern:

1 → 2 → 3 → 1 → 2 → 3 → 4 → 5

Notes: C D E F G A B C
