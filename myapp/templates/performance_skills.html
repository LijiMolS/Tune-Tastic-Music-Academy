Performance Skills

Build confidence by improving rhythm, dynamics and expressive playing.
Learn to perform smoothly and musically.

1. Play from Memory

  • Practice small sections repeatedly
  • Close your eyes and recall notes
  • Build muscle memory

2. Dynamics (Soft & Loud)

  • Play softly (piano) and loudly (forte)
  • Add emotion to your playing
  • Control finger pressure

3. Metronome Practice

  • Practice with steady timing
  • Start slow, increase speed gradually
  • Improve rhythm accuracy
